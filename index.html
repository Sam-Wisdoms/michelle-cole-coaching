What We Do

Personalized Emotional, Practical and Self-Care Support Strategies

Having ever been where our clients are, we've got you entirely covered knowing exactly what it means to be where you are. We offer Assistance in preparing for the transition from the neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) to home We offer One-on-one and tailored coaching sessions to help parents cope with the emotional impact of having a premature baby. We also offer personalized self-care plans to help parents prioritize their well-being amidst the stress of caring for a premature baby. We create awareness among them by providing guidance about how they can manage stress and promote mental and emotional resilience.

Community Connection and Developmental Monitoring

In seasons of difficulty, what keeps us strong most of the time is supportive community. We offer community-building events and connect you with support groups, so that you are not all alone trying to navigate the season. To ensurthat are not alone, we offer group support sessions that connect parents who share similar experiences, fostering a sense of community and understanding. We also do regular check-ins to monitor the developmental progress of premature infants and provide ongoing guidance and support for a smooth transition from the NICU to home. These services are to ensure a smooth adjustment for both parents and their premature infants.

Informational Guidance, Parenting Skills Development, Advocacy and Communication Skills

To ensure that our clients are equipped with the right information, we provide them with resources on premature baby care, developmental milestones, and potential challenges that they may encounter. We also organize workshops and webinars led by medical experts, covering topics such as neonatal care, feeding strategies, and developmental stimulation. We do personalized coaching to enhance parenting skills specifically tailored for premature infants as well as offering ractical guidance on feeding techniques, sleep routines, and developmental activities that are suitable for premature babies. To ensure that our clients are confident, we equip them with communication skills and relevant information, by coaching them on how they can build good relationship with medical profesionals and also about how they can advocate for their rights.
