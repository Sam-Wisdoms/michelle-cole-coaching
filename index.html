What We Do

Emotional Support Sessions: One-on-one coaching sessions to help parents cope with the emotional impact of having a premature baby. Group support sessions to connect parents who share similar experiences, fostering a sense of community and understanding. Informational Guidance: Educational resources on premature baby care, developmental milestones, and potential challenges. Workshops and webinars led by medical experts, covering topics such as neonatal care, feeding strategies, and developmental stimulation. Parenting Skills Development: Personalized coaching to enhance parenting skills specifically tailored for premature infants. Practical guidance on feeding techniques, sleep routines, and developmental activities suitable for premature babies. Advocacy and Communication Skills: Coaching on effective communication with healthcare professionals, ensuring parents feel confident in advocating for their child's needs. Assistance in navigating the healthcare system, understanding medical jargon, and accessing support networks. Self-Care Strategies: Personalized self-care plans to help parents prioritize their well-being amidst the stress of caring for a premature baby. Mindfulness and relaxation techniques to manage stress and promote mental and emotional resilience. Community Building Events: Organizing events, both online and offline, to foster a sense of community among parents facing similar challenges. Support groups and social gatherings to share experiences, advice, and encouragement. Developmental Monitoring and Guidance: Regular check-ins to assess the developmental progress of premature infants. Tailored advice and activities to support the unique needs of premature babies at each stage of development. Transition to Home Support: Assistance in preparing for the transition from the neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) to home. Home care strategies and resources to ensure a smooth adjustment for both parents and their premature infants. Online Resource Hub: Access to a comprehensive online resource hub with articles, videos, and downloadable materials addressing various aspects of premature baby care and parenting.
